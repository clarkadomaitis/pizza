PIZZA OUTDOOR DINING

Pizza Places in Marine Park Are Participating in Indoor Dining, For the Time Being...

Marine Park is a neighborhood in southeast Brooklyn. The neighborhood is home to seven pizza places.

Indoor dining was made legal in NYC on September 30, after over 6 months of being shut down. All seven pizza places have decided to open tables for indoor dining.

Marine Park is in one of the 11 zip codes 11 zip codes that Mayor Bill de Blasio announced on October 4 that are seeing increasing COVID-19 infections. The current 14-day positivity rate is 2.22% in the 11234 zip code. If Marine Park's numbers rise over 3%, they may see a shutdown of indoor dining and outdoor dining.
